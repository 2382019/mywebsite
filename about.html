My name is Gisella Mercy Waworuntu, but people usually just call me Gisel. I was born in Bekasi on 2nd September 2005 and spent most of my life in the rainy city of Bogor.

I graduated from Adventist University of Indonesia (UNAI) in Parongpong, Bandung, where I actively participated in organizations like Witnessing Sound Choir and HIMA Faculty of Technology of Information UNAI.

As a fresh graduate, I bring extensive experience in UI/UX design, driven by my passion for creating impactful and user-friendly digital experiences.
